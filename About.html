PhD research.
My PhD thesis concerns the domain of Machine Learning and aim
at designing Decision Tree algorithms adapted to handle with
large temporal dataset. The goal is to improve the temporal decision
tree in term of computational complexity, performance and interpretability.
This PhD thesis is part of IKATS project that is a Research and Development
project founded by the french government in the frame of PIA program.
